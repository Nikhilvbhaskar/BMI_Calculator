What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It's a measure that uses an individual's weight and height to estimate body fat and categorize them as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. BMI is calculated by dividing weight in kilograms by height in meters squared (kg/mÂ²). It's a simple and widely used tool for assessing weight status and potential health risks associated with weight.
